about me

Hi! I'm Sanjanaa Sridhar, a Master’s in Information Management student at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. My interest began in undergrad through sensors and signal processing, when I grew curious about the data behind systems. That led me to a Research Internship at NUS and I haven't looked back since then. I love working with messy datasets; cleaning, analyzing, modeling, and uncovering patterns to support better decisions.

Outside of data and tech, dancing is my first love -I'm a trained Bharatanatyam dancer, but more than the training, it's the joy and freedom it brings me that I cherish most. I also love getting my hands messy with painting and pottery, curling up with my journal, or spending quiet hours crocheting. I truly believe that learning doesn’t just happen in classrooms or through code, it comes from all the little experiences in life. I'm always curious, always exploring, and constantly looking for new ways to grow.
